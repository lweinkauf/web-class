Chapter 1. London

London was my home from the months of January 2023 to May 2023. I had no idea what to expect when coming in, all I knew to be prepared for was rain. London was the place that helped my transition from child to adult. Who I was before living in London and who I came out after are two completely different individuals. I became more confident, independent, free-spirited, and open-minded. I fell completely in love with the city, something captivating me at every corner. I will forever be incredibly thankful for this place, for the people I met, relationships I built, places I traveled to, and friends who became family.


Highlights

1. My Art and Architecture class that gave me the opportunity to go to many of London's museums and travel around new areas of the city
2. The week my parents visited and taking them to Brighton and Seven Sisters Country Park
3. Living with my 5 best friends in a really sweet area of London near campus called Clerkenwell, my favorite street in the city will always be Exmouth Market, a block around the corner from our apartment full of charming small restraunts, shops, and people.
