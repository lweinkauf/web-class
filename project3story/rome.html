Chapter 2. Rome

Rome was the first place outside of London that my friends traveled to as a group during our time in Europe. We were going to visit a few people from Fordham who were studying there so we invited some of our guy friends, which was the start of a group of 12 of us becoming extremely close and going everywhere together. I remember waking up at 4:30 in the morning to walk to the train that brought us to the airport, the anxiety I had about my second flight without my parents, and the anticipation for my first time in Italy. Whenever I look back on this trip I feel an overwhelming sense of gratitude. I had the opportunity to go to the Vatican, inside the Sistine Chapel, see the Colosseum, make a wish at the Trevi Fountain and walk circles around the city of Rome. It was peacful to feel at home so far away from New York.
